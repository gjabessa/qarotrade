    We are Qaro Coffee

    Qaro farm or better to say it community is an area located in Godere woreda, Gambela region. The area is covered with coffee trees and dense natural forests. Qaro means coffee in Mezhenger language, an ethnic group located in the northwestern part of the region. The Mezhenger discovered the area while they traveled for hunting. They saw the area vastly covered with coffee trees and named it Qaro.

    In 2011 the plantation established on 365 hectares of land located in Godere, Gambela region. It is managed and led by professionals with background in coffee farming.

    We started harvesting in 2016. Now we have annual production of over 300 MT coffees. In order to increase our coffee volume, we are communicating with the region investment office to expand the farm size to 1,000 hectares. Recently, the region investment office has ranked our company among the top investment companies in the region who have demonstrated high performance & development. We are also working with farmers (out growers) in the surrounding area to process and market their coffee through our plantation.

    The company is a member of Ethiopian Coffee Growers and Experters Association and Addis Ababa CHamber of Commerce.
