    About Qaro Coffee

    Our company started development of the plantation in 2011 with 355 hectares of land it received from Gambela Investment Office. We started harvesting in 2016.

    Now we have annual production of over 300 MT coffees. In order to increase our coffee volume, we are communicating with the region investment office to expand the farm size to 1,000 hectares. Recently, the region investment office has ranked our company among the top investment companies in the region who have demonstrated high performance & development.
